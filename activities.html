This page highlights selected conferences, workshops, and professional activities related to my research in semiconductor materials for photovoltaic and infrared applications.

🏆 Best Oral Presentation Award — MRS Spring 2025

Awarded Best Oral Presentation in Symposium EN05: Thin-Film Compound Semiconductor Photovoltaics at the Materials Research Society (MRS) Spring Meeting 2025. This work focused on optoelectronic properties of thin-film CaZn2P2 and SrZn2P2 including doping mechanisms for next-generation tandem solar cells.
